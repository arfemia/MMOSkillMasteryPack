Restores the Mastery Point level rewards, which were silently missing for every skill.

- **Fixed: the +1 Mastery Point reward at every 15th level never appeared.** The reward template was filed under a name the reward list could not match, so no skill ever offered its mastery-point milestones. (The Mastery Points currency itself was unaffected, which is why points earned elsewhere still spent on nodes.) Every built-in skill again grants a claimable Mastery Point every 15 levels, from 15 up to 195. Existing per-skill `command-rewards.json` overrides are unaffected.
